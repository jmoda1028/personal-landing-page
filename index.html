About

Welcome to my mini portfolio website! My name is Junaid Moda and most of my friends call me jun for short. I'm a web designer and currently staying in Manila, Philippines. I'm a person who has high passion in design technologies. One of my dreams is to master all the design technologies and become one of the top web designers in the world, and now, I'm working toward it!

I'm Enthusiastic and self-motivated web designer. A highly-motivated graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Ama Computer College with 1 year of experience by using React.js. Eager to learn more to bring top-class frontend development.

I believe that great design is in the details. I also believe that design must support functionality, and vice versa. One can't, and shouldn't be used to overcome the weaknesess of the other. Design is more than just choosing colors, typefaces, shapes, and images. Good design works to present information in such a way as to support the information it represents.

When I design a web site, I'm constantly considering how the site will be built and how it will function. This way, you can be sure that both design and usability will be considered along every step of the way.
